Frequently Asked Questions

We're Here to Help

Find answers to common questions about traveling with Riris Tours

How do I book a tour with Riris Tours?

Booking with us is simple! You can contact us directly via email at info@riristours.com or call us at +30 210 1234567. Our travel specialists will work with you to create a customized itinerary that matches your preferences, budget, and travel dates. We handle all the details from accommodation to activities.

What is included in the tour packages?

Our tour packages typically include accommodation in carefully selected hotels, daily breakfast, guided tours with expert local guides, airport transfers, and all entrance fees to attractions. Specific inclusions vary by package, and we'll provide you with a detailed itinerary outlining everything that's covered.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 60 days or more before departure receive a full refund minus a $200 administrative fee. Cancellations between 30-59 days before departure receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 30 days before departure are non-refundable. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect your investment.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 10 or more travelers. Group tours can also be fully customized to your specifications. Contact us for a personalized quote and to discuss your group's unique needs and interests.

What languages do your guides speak?

Our professional guides are multilingual and fluent in English, Greek, French, German, Italian, and Spanish. When you book your tour, let us know your preferred language and we'll ensure you're paired with a guide who speaks it fluently.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is not automatically included in our packages, but we highly recommend it and can help you purchase comprehensive coverage. Travel insurance protects you against unexpected events like trip cancellations, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more.

Can I customize my itinerary?

Absolutely! Customization is one of our specialties. We can adjust any aspect of your tour including destinations, accommodation level, activities, pace, and duration. Our goal is to create the perfect journey tailored specifically to your interests and travel style.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and PayPal. A deposit of 30% is required to confirm your booking, with the remaining balance due 60 days before departure.
