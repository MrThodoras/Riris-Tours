About Riris Tours

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Riris Tours was born from a passion for exploration and a deep love for the world's most captivating destinations. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most respected travel agencies in the Mediterranean, known for crafting bespoke journeys that transform the way people experience the world.

Our philosophy is simple: travel should be transformative, authentic, and unforgettable. We believe in creating experiences that go beyond the typical tourist trail, connecting you with the heart and soul of each destination. Whether you're seeking the sun-drenched islands of Greece, the romantic streets of Paris, or the exotic landscapes of Asia, we curate every detail with precision and care.
